Publication Details

Proceedings on Engineering Sciences, a Scopus-indexed journal, have agreed to publish papers from GREENCON 2025, subject to meeting their quality requirements.
A select number of papers will be published in a special issue of Talanta Open (a Scopus-indexed journal with an impact factor of 4.2), provided they meet the journal's quality standards and fall within its scope. The APC charges will be in accordance with the journal's guidelines.

  • Any manuscript submitted to GREENCON 2025 must be original, and its content, or substantial portions of it, must not be under consideration or previously published by any other journal.
  • All submissions to GREENCON 2025 will be evaluated by the chairs of the respective tracks, who will determine their suitability for peer review based on the following criteria:
    • The similarity index, verified by a professional plagiarism checker, should be less than 15%.
    • The relevance, timeliness, and significance of the topic.
  • The peer review process involves double blinded evaluation of manuscripts by two independent reviewers.
  • The technical committee will make a decision on the acceptance of the manuscript for presentation based on the reviewer comments. These comments will be shared with the authors for necessary modifications.
  • The publication process will require a minimum of 6 to 8 months from the date of the conference.
